Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information about you when you use the Game, including:

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifier (IDFA) on iOS devices. This information is used to show you personalized ads and to measure the performance of those ads. You can opt-out of targeted advertising by disabling the "Limit Ad Tracking" feature in your device settings.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your usage of the Game, such as the features you use and the content you access. We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data and understand how users interact with the Game. The data we collect includes crash reports, user experience data, and location data. Firebase Analytics's privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy respectively.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect about you to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Game.
  • Personalize your experience and present relevant content and advertising.
  • Analyze and understand how people use the Game using Firebase Analytics.

Sharing of Information

We share information about you with:

  • Advertising partners: We use AdMob as our advertising provider and may share your IDFA with them to show you personalized ads and measure the performance of those ads. AdMob's privacy policy can be found at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en
  • We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data and understand how users interact with the Game. Firebase Analytics's privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request that your data be deleted. To do so, you can delete your Firebase account
