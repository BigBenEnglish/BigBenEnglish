  • Device Type: We may collect information about your device type (e.g. iPhone X, Samsung Galaxy S21) to help us improve the performance of our app across different devices.
  • Operating System: We may collect information about the operating system (e.g. iOS 15, Android 12) that you are using in order to optimize the app's performance for that platform.
  • Crash Logs: Our third-party services may collect crash logs when the app experiences errors or crashes. This information is used to improve app stability and performance.
  • App Event Logs: We may collect information about how you use our app, including which features you use and how often you use them. This information is used to improve the app and provide a better user experience.
  • User Language: We may collect information about the language you are using on your device in order to provide localized content within the app.
  • Location Data: Our app does not collect precise location data, but we may collect general geographic information about our users, such as their city or country, through third-party services like Firebase Analytics. This information is used to help us understand the distribution of our user base and serve localized content and advertisements.
